RFC Process

Fred's RFC Process:

  1. Idea: Fred has a brilliant (or questionable) idea
  2. Coffee: Fred drinks coffee and thinks about it more
  3. Draft: Fred writes the RFC in his basement
  4. Review: Community reviews and comments
  5. More Coffee: Fred considers feedback
  6. Decision: Fred makes the final call
